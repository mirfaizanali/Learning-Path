Study Plan
Your 3-Month Study Plan
Phase 01
AI & ML Foundations
Weeks 1–3 · ~3 hrs/wk

Build the conceptual vocabulary the exam expects. As a Java dev, you'll grasp these quickly. Focus on understanding what the services do — not how to build them. Covers Domain 1 (20%) fully.

AI vs ML vs Deep Learning Supervised / Unsupervised / Reinforcement Neural Networks basics Training vs Inference SageMaker overview Amazon Rekognition Comprehend + Transcribe Bias & variance
🎯 Milestone: Score 75%+ on a Domain 1 section test (Tutorials Dojo)
Phase 02
Generative AI & Foundation Models
Weeks 4–7 · ~3 hrs/wk

The highest-weighted section of the exam (52% combined). Dive into GenAI concepts, prompt engineering, Amazon Bedrock, and how Foundation Models are applied. This is also where your Java dev career gets exciting — you'll learn to call Bedrock APIs.

LLMs & Foundation Models Prompt Engineering Amazon Bedrock Bedrock Knowledge Bases RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) Amazon Q Fine-tuning vs RAG tradeoffs Agents for Bedrock Token limits & cost
🎯 Milestone: Build a simple Bedrock API call in Java (free tier) + score 75%+ on Domains 2 & 3
Phase 03
Responsible AI & Governance
Weeks 8–9 · ~2 hrs/wk

Covers Domains 4 & 5 (28% combined). Often underestimated — exam questions here are scenario-based and need clear thinking. Lighter than earlier phases but important for the score.

Fairness & Bias in AI Explainability (SageMaker Clarify) Transparency & Accountability AWS AI Service Cards IAM for AI workloads VPC & data encryption for AI Compliance frameworks Model monitoring
🎯 Milestone: Read the AWS Responsible AI whitepaper + score 75%+ on Domains 4 & 5
Phase 04
Full Exam Simulation & Final Review
Weeks 10–12 · ~3 hrs/wk

Consolidate everything with timed full-length mock exams. Analyse wrong answers, revisit weak domains, and do a final speed-read of the AWS exam guide cheat sheet before booking your slot.

Full mock exams (timed) Wrong-answer analysis Weak domain re-study AWS Exam Guide re-read Flash card blitz Exam logistics prep
🎯 Milestone: Achieve 80%+ on two consecutive full-length mocks, then book the exam
